MY CHARACTERS

Meet some of the characters that you will find in the game:


Traveller

The traveller, a knight of great importance to the kingdom of Laratis, who has been captured by the opposing army, the warriors of Mawel. This will be the character you play as throughout the game as you unlock new weapons and abilities to escape the fortress of Sanguise.

David, the Prisoner

David was once part of a group of warriors that is now long lost in history, cursed with a long life span, he now spends his days rotting in a cell, with his morality and spirit crushed. This is the first boss that you will fight in the game, his wild swings and erratic movement are sure to be a challenge.

Dolor, the Executioner(In Progress)

Dolor is a bloodthirsty killer, it is his job, however his methods are what made him famous. He makes sure to use the bluntest weapon possible during an execution, letting the victim feel it all. However this may lead to his downfall.

Grand Knight Victoris(In Progress)

Victoris is undefeated, a true game changer who is capable to turn the tide of battle on his lonesome. He is feared by all who know him due to his power. None could beat him....until now.

My Work

My game is a survival/roguelike game where you have been imprisoned and must escape the dungeon of the castle you were trapped in. The game will be made for audiences between the ages of 10 and 16. In my game there will be multiple enemy types, like an evil knight and a magethat shoots fire, as well as multiple bosses that consist of 3 of the characters above. These being the prisoner, the executioner, and the grand knight.

Main Menu

From the main menu you will be able to enter the game, exit the game, and personalise your settings.

First Levels

In the first levels you will find out the controls and the main motive of the game. You will fight basic enemies and gather keys to unlock the door to the first boss of the game, the prisoner.

Boss Room 1

This is the room where you will fight the prisoner boss, he has been stuck in his cell for what seems like forever, which has driven him mad.
